--- Basic usage ---

Text with period:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello.")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>

Text with question mark:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello?")
    Hello?
    <BLANKLINE>

Text with colon:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello:")
    Hello:
    <BLANKLINE>

Multiple special characters:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello. How are you? Fine: thanks.")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    How are you?
    <BLANKLINE>
    Fine:
    <BLANKLINE>
    thanks.
    <BLANKLINE>

--- No special characters ---

Plain text with no special characters:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello world")
    Hello world

--- Spaces handling ---

No leading spaces on new line:
    >>> text_indentation("Hello.   World.")
    Hello.
    <BLANKLINE>
    World.
    <BLANKLINE>
